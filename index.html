About Carl

I'm Carl Shawn

The streets of Chicago's inner city raised me. They taught me about adversity, about disappointment, and about the grit it takes to overcome.

I'm not just talking theory; I lived it. I saw the struggles, but I also saw the hustles, the unwavering determination that makes you rise up when the world expects you to fall. That fire for resilience was forged on those blocks, and it's what fueled my purpose to flip the legal profession on its head.

Forget the stuffy traditions. I'm here to tell you that true strength isn't hiding behind a polished facade. It's found in the power of vulnerability, in the raw, real conversations that build genuine connection.

Our Philosophy

Flipping the Script on Legal Leadership

At Carl Shawn Watkins Consulting, we're rewriting the playbook. Forget everything you've been told about what makes a great leader. It ain't just about authority, big titles, or what's on your resume. We believe true power comes from being real.

In the cutthroat, high-stakes legal world, effective leadership isn't just about being tough; it's about being courageous enough to be human. We're not your average consultants. We're here to help legal professionals and firms unlock that next-level potential by embracing vulnerability as a superpower, not a weakness.

We dismantle the old-school armor and build up a culture of real talk, trust, and straight-up collaboration. That's where the real magic happens.
