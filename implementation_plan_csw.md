# Implementation Plan

### Objective

I am building a website for my new client, Carl Shawn Watkins Consulting LLC https://carlshawnwatkins.com/

Carl Shawn Watkins Consulting provides leadership coaching and professional development services tailored specifically for legal professionals, focusing on helping attorneys and law firm teams build authentic, empathetic, and effective leadership skills rather than just practicing law. Their offerings include one-on-one coaching, group executive coaching, vulnerability-based leadership programs, keynote speaking, and tailored strategic support that encourages lawyers to lead with confidence, resilience, and authenticity.

They work with individual attorneys, law firm partners, legal teams, and legal organizations seeking to elevate their leadership abilities, cultivate inclusive and high-performing workplace cultures, and transform professional challenges into opportunities for growth. The firm emphasizes a human-first, vulnerability-driven approach to leadership that fosters collaboration, trust, and lasting personal and professional development within the legal sector.

The goal of this website is to showcase my client Carl Shawn Watkins Consulting as a reputable, professional, and effective leadership coaching partner that a prospective client should work with, and to provide clear CTAs for a prospective client to connect with Carl Shawn Watkins Consulting about engaging their legal leadership, coaching, and professional development services.

### Design

Redesign this landing page to look like it was built by a top-tier modern design agency charging $50k+. Focus on:

- Subtle, sophisticated animations (micro-interactions, smooth transitions)
- A cohesive, premium color palette (avoid anything that feels "template-y")
- Polished details: shadows, gradients, borders that feel intentional and high-end
- Visual rhythm and alignment that feels considered
- Please do animation on every number on each page from 0 and count up when it scrolls into view
- Use refined typography that aligns with the company’s industry and brand identity

The only constraints are to use the attached logo in the navbar, apply fonts suited for a modern layout, follow the original website’s color theme, and ensure any hero stock imagery aligns with their core services or products.

### Copywriting

Take full liberty with the copywriting for this website. In the client's own words, this is how they describe themselves and their services. This information needs to be conveyed in a way that is marketed and comes across well to the user on the website.
